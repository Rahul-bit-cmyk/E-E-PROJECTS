5. Glossary of Terms

PLC (Programmable Logic Controller)

Industrial digital computer designed for automated control of manufacturing processes.

SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition)

System for remote monitoring and control of industrial processes.

PROFINET

Industrial Ethernet standard for automation.
